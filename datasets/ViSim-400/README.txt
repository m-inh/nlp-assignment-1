ViSim-400 is a Vietnamese dataset of semantic relation pairs for evaluation of models that reflect the continuum between similarity and relatedness.

# Dataset Description # 

ViSim-400.txt file is a tab separated plaintext file, where rows corresponding to concept pairs and  columns corresponding to properties of each pair.

- Word1: The first concept in the pair.

- Word2: The second concept in the pair.

- POS: The part-of-speech of the pair.

- Sim1: The similarity rating in the range [0,6] which is the average score of 15 raters.

- Sim2: The similarity rating in the range [0,10] which is mapped from the range [0,6] to the range [0,10].

- STD: The standard deviation of annotator scores when rating this pair.
